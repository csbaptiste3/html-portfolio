Candace's Favorite Movies by Genre

Top 3 Romantic Films

Dirty Dancing

I recall being a young girl and sneaking to watch this film. Dancing was forbidden in my family and to see Johnny Castle teach Baby to break out of her shell was amazing!

Pretty Woman

Edward Lewis looking past Vivian's stature/profession and seeing who she truly was to her core made me fall in love with the possibility of love.

Beyond The Lights

Kazaam rescuing and overcoming his fears for the woman he loves. Challenging her to see all her potential. Enough said.
